The Cornell Legacy



In 1975 April Cornell fell in love with India's rich culture, igniting a lifelong passion for textiles and design. In 1992 she founded Cornell Overseas, a company dedicated to producing apparel and textiles beloved the world over. Specializing in print and cotton, Cornell Overseas has grown to be a trusted supplier for boutique and leading global brands. For over thirty years, this woman-run company has not only been a source of beauty for our world but has also built a ladder of opportunity for our dedicated employees. We invite you to experience the beauty and craftsmanship of India, as April herself discovered so many years ago.

Namaste, April and family
